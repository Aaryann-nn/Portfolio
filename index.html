01 — About

Behind
the lens

Aaryan Kewalia

Photographer
& Storyteller

I'm Aaryan Kewalia, a photographer and visual storyteller based in Ahmedabad. Through my lens, I document live performances, everyday life, and the people who shape the stories around us.

My work is driven by a curiosity for authentic moments — whether it's the energy of a concert stage, the character of a city street, or a fleeting human expression. As a Journalism and Mass Communication student, I believe photography is more than creating images.
